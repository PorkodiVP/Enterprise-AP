WHAT IS BUSINESS ARCHITECTURE AND STANDARDIZATION?

  • The core of establishing a Business Architecture team within Adenza is about enabling a new vision for strategy execution and creating a new capability within the org to enact change. The change is connecting what we sell to what we build to what we deliver.

  • With a passion and ability to think big & full picture, synthesize and abstract, architect and design, communicate with creativity and influence, the Business Architecture team works upstream in the strategy execution life cycle. They build the business architecture knowledgebase and create maps and models that focus on applying business architecture to bring value.
